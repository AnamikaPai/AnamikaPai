For android users, 
1. Navigate to "Application search" on your phone 
2. Select " Calculator" app and the app will open up
3. For basic arithmetic operations {Add (+) , Subtract (-) , multiply (x) , Divide ( /)} , use the number keys and mathematical operators that you see on your screen. 
4. To obtain the result , use '=' 
5. To erase stepwise , use <- key 
6. For advanced arithmetic operations, click on ^. Refer section 2 for advanced arithemntic operations
